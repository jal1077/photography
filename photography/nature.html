Photos of Nature

I took this picture when it was a Kate Spade event in Gansevoort Market. That event was quick and fun; the weather was also perfect. Not too cold or too hot. I had so much fun with my friend. Since apples are my favorite fruit, I was attracted to take a picture of the apple tree.

While walking to class, it was pouring hard. There was a big rainstorm that made the street look almost magical. The rain gracefully poured on the trees, and the street was peaceful. If only I took a short video of what I saw. The picture didn't do justice to the rainy scene.

As I was going on the road that day, the sky reminded me of a bit like cotton candy. It was mainly blue, but you still saw the cotton candy pink hues in the sky. The view was majestic.

We were coming from the mountains, and we had to stop. The background was breathtaking. The sky looked incredible: mainly grey and blue but with wisps of white. The asphalt road was a great contrast to the green trees and vegetation.
