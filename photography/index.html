What defines a photographer?

When I started out taking pictures, I had no goals. I took pictures because it was fun, and it captured the moments that I wanted to remember. But as more people noticed that I took pictures and was passionate about taking pictures, they started to ask, "Can I see your photos? What are you going to do with those photos?" And that was the moment I began to question everything.

What should I do with my photos?

Do I show them to people?

Do I let them stay be unknown?

But the more I thought of it, the more I realized I should show them. I should be proud of my photos.
